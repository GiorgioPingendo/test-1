Are there any trial?

Go with the free plan, it's full featured.

How do I subscribe to repos?

From the account page check/uncheck the repos you want to activate/deactivate. Deactivate keeps the repo  working till the end of the paid period.

What about payments?

Payments are managed by Fastspring that accepts all major credit cards and Paypal.

How billing works?

Billing is per repo x month.  Each one is billed separately accordingly to its activation date. 

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, just deactivate all privates repos from your account page.

Should i pay Github also  for private ?

Yes, just deactivate all privates repos from your account page.
