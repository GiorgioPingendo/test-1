Developers and non developers, what's different?

Github collaboration

Pingendo's collaboration is built on top of Github. Github knowledges is required to set up a collaboration project. 

Editor

The editor features non dev and dev features. Users will remain on the surface or will go deep according to their role and skill. Everybody will benefit of perceiving how their contribution will look and feel in the page.

  • A copywriter edits texts.
  • A photographer updates links to photos.
  • A designer will change colours or move components and sections.
  • A SEO specialist will will add page title, description and more... 
  • A marketer will act where he/she can and distributes tasks.
  • A stakeholder can be a main contributor or just be set as read only and asked to approve the job at required stages.
  • A developer will focus on issues the above people can't. e.g. effects, custom responsive rules, deep styling...

What is a prorated billing?

Proration makes handles the increase or decrease to the subscription price when the number of activated repositories varies during the month of subscription. If you add 1 repository for 10 days only, you'll pay it immediately, and we'll credit 20 days on your next billing period. more...

Can I cancel any time?

Of course. There are no obligations or contracts. You can cancel at any time in the billing section of the Dashboard. Once you cancel your account, you will not be charged again.
