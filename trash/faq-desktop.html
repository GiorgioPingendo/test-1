Pingendo - FAQ

What is Pingendo ?


Pingendo is a Bootstrap builder for Mac, Windows and Linux.
It's simple and generates only vanilla Bootstrap 4 HTML and SASS/CSS.

Who is it for ?


  It's for developers who work with Bootstrap and want to be more productive.
It's also a great tool for learners.

How much does it costs ?

Pingendo is free during beta.

Is there an online version?

Yes, Pingendo for Github, great for teams.

So, where to start ?

Download beta version from www.pingendo.com/v4/
