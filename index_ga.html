Teamwork, great websites, smooth iteration process, dev, non dev as marcom and stakeholders,  each one its tool                           
 

Developers

Developers have the privilege to iterate on a real page showing in the browser. 
Pingendo extend this feature to non-devs.
Thus, they become more independent and take care of wording themselves, relying on dev for coding only.

Non developers

Clients, stakeholders, designers, copywriters and marketers are by far the group who spend the largest amount of time iterating on a website.
We take them in the browser, iterating in front of the real page, side by side with developers.
No more confined to offline tools such as emails, docs, psd, mockups.

Together

Pingendo is for teams where at least one dev is involved. 
Dev has two clear tasks: coding and manage everyone and everything on Github, the most adopted revision control system.
Non developers will be enabled to iterate much more independently.

We created two dedicated tools to keep all of them in the loop.
